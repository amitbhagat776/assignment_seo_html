Favicon

A favicon, short for "favorite icon," is a small icon associated with a website or webpage. It is typically displayed in the browser's address bar, tabs, bookmarks, and in the browser's history to help users quickly identify a website. Favicons are usually square in shape and are typically 16x16 pixels or 32x32 pixels in size, although larger sizes are often used for higher resolution displays.

When you have a list of open tabs in your browser, the favicons help you to quickly identify the different web pages. Favicons are also displayed in the bookmark bar and history list, making it easier to find the websites that you visit frequently.
